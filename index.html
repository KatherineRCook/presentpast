PresentPast Design Lab is an emerging freelance design firm focussed on innovative and thoughtful digital applications for heritage, arts and community endeavours.

PresentPAST is grounded in the museum world, in Canada and internationally; our education, work experience and passion hinges on presenting the past in engaging and sustainable ways.

Our background means that we understand the contemporary context, issues and needs of 21st century museums.
